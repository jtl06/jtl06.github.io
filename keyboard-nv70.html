NV70

Built in 2023 with an NVIDIA employee celebrating his team’s GPU wins, NV70 embraces a southpaw layout milled from anodized 6063 aluminum and electrophoresis-coated stainless 310. The top-mount chassis became my testbed for firmware abstractions tuned to mirrored numpads and custom daughterboards.
