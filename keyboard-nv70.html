NV70

The NV70 was designed as a followup to the LS70, to celebrate a longtime Nvidia employee's company success and promotion. It features a unique set of themed aluminum machined keycaps.

  • 2023
  • 70% Layout
  • Top mount
  • 6063 Al · stainless 310
