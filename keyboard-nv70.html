NV70

Built in 2023 alongside an NVIDIA engineer, NV70 celebrates the company’s GPU success with a southpaw layout milled from anodized 6063 aluminum and electrophoresis-coated stainless 310. The collaboration doubled as a proving ground for firmware abstractions tailored to mirrored numpads.

  • 2023
  • Southpaw 70%
  • Top-mount
  • 6063 Al + stainless 310
  • Custom daughterboard

Why southpaw?

Moving the numpad left frees mouse space and forces careful layer planning. NV70 helped validate my firmware abstractions for mirrored layouts used in accessibility-focused builds.
