LS70

A low-profile 70% board machined from aluminum with a layered internal weight system. LS70 experiments with south-facing LEDs, a custom plate cut, and a gasket isolation pattern tuned to even out signature frequencies across the layout.

  • 70% layout
  • Brass weight stack
  • Gasket mount
  • Dark navy anodize

Firmware notes

LS70 runs QMK with layers customized for firmware testing. Southpaw macro layers make it a perfect daily driver when validating USB HID and tap-hold behavior across firmware builds.
