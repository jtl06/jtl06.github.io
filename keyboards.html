Keyboard builds

A side hobby that keeps my hands grounded while I work through architecture diagrams. Each build balances acoustics, switch selection, and firmware experimentation.

LS70

Low-slung 70% board exploring layered weight design, polished by a deep navy anodize and brass accents.

Aria TKL

Tenkeyless workhorse tuned for a balanced plate flex and even acoustics across the alphas and nav cluster.

NV70

Southpaw 70% concept with a left-hand numpad layout—built to stress-test custom firmware layers.

Hydaelyn

Final Fantasy-inspired theme with stepped blockers and frosted polycarbonate diffuser for soft underglow.

EXT65

Exploratory 65% with extended nav keys, focusing on CNC tolerances and exposed fastener aesthetics.

Koi65

Artisan koi engraving paired with a serene teal finish; a canvas for experimenting with switch lube profiles.
