Koi65

Completed in 2023, Koi65 channels the calm of koi ponds through sweeping engravings, anodized 6063 aluminum, and C110 copper weight work. The sandwich-gasket 65% layout was designed as a paired set and tuned for long-form writing sessions with medium tactiles, a dampened polycarbonate plate, and a stainless 304 variant.

Sound profile

The polycarbonate plate and poron foam produce a muted, rounded sound signature—perfect for late-night firmware debugging without distracting teammates on call. A stainless 304 weight variant tightens the sound further while showcasing a mirror-polished finish.
