About me

I’m an undergraduate ECE student at the University of Washington, Seattle, splitting time between custom firmware work and trail runs with my dog Mocha. Building precise, tactile keyboards funds my hardware experiments and keeps me grounded in craftsmanship.

Outside of labs you’ll find me chasing golden hour for bird photography, tinkering with Detroit-style pizza recipes, or leveling up in Valorant (Ascendant III). Those hobbies keep my eyes sharp and my reflexes honest — handy when debugging oscilloscopes at 2 a.m.

What I do

I design firmware, embedded tooling, and measurement platforms. Recent highlights include a high-density temperature monitoring system for NIST’s tape tunnel chamber and the Better Latency Tester (BLT) — a click-to-photon benchmarking rig built on STM32. I have experiance with instrumentats such as oscilloscopes and logic analyzers, as well as logic design on FPGA.

How I work

I’m pragmatic but detail-oriented: start with measured requirements, prototype quickly, instrument everything, and write documentation. Whether I’m coordinating cross-division reviews at NIST or negotiating overseas production for Aeus, I keep feedback loops short and transparency high.

Experience

  • NIST Sensor Science Division · 2023 — Built a Raspberry Pi data acquisition platform with 61 I²C sensors, uncovering five hotspot regions and cutting chamber temperature uncertainty by 38%.
  • Aeus Keyboards · 2020 – Present — Founded a custom keyboard studio (> $20K revenue), designing ATMega32U4 PCBs, writing C firmware, managing overseas manufacturing, and hand-assembling SMD prototypes.

Skills snapshot

C/C++, ARM & RISC-V assembly, Python, SystemVerilog, MATLAB; Git/GitHub, GCC, GDB, Quartus, Vivado, ModelSim; USB FS, DMA-driven ADC, UART/I²C/SPI/GPIO, timers/ISRs, JTAG, CRC, Zephyr RTOS, Linux/WSL.

Beyond the desk

I enjoy the outdoors, with bird photography and hiking. I also enjoy cooking, with my favorite dish being Detroit style pizza. I also enjoy gaming, and keep a competitive edge by playing Valorant (Rank: Ascendant III).
