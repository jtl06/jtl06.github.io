Aria TKL

Built in 2022, Aria TKL pairs anodized 6063 aluminum, stainless 304, and brass to deliver a rigid-yet-balanced platform for firmware prototyping. The leaf-spring plate and o-ring isolation keep acoustics consistent while I capture data for switch and stabilizer experiments.

  • 2022
  • Tenkeyless
  • O-ring isolation
  • 6063 Al · stainless 304 · brass
