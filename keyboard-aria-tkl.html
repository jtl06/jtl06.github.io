Aria TKL

Aria TKL is my daily driver for firmware prototyping. The leaf-spring plate and o-ring isolation deliver a consistent typing feel while giving room to isolate switch and stabilizer acoustics for recorded comparisons.

  • Tenkeyless layout
  • Leaf-spring plate
  • O-ring isolation
  • Frost silver finish

Layout experiments

Aria TKL splits its function row and nav cluster across firmware layers, letting me validate tap-hold timings and sticky key strategies used in BLT testing workflows.
