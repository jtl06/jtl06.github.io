ARMv8 microarchitecture study

A SystemVerilog CPU study moving from single-cycle execution to a 5-stage pipeline.

Implemented an ARM-style RISC CPU with hazard detection and forwarding. Integrated a direct-mapped cache and used ModelSim benches to measure CPI impacts from branch penalties and cache misses.
