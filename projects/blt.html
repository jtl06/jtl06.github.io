BLT latency measurement platform

STM32 input-to-photon latency measurement device for benchmarking gaming PC responsiveness.

Built around an STM32 Nucleo board, BLT combines USB HID emulation, timers, circular DMA, and ISRs for microsecond-level timing.

  • Quantified DMA ISR jitter at ±8 microseconds at 2.5 kHz.
  • Scripted OpenOCD and GDB flash/debug workflow with Make.
  • Built Python GUI for data collection and CSV export.
