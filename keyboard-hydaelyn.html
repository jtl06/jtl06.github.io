Hydaelyn

Inspired by Final Fantasy XIV’s Hydaelyn arc, this board mixes frosted polycarbonate, polished blockers, and diffuse per-key lighting. It’s tuned for softer acoustics that complement late-night firmware work sessions.

  • Alice-inspired
  • Frosted diffuser
  • Tray mount
  • Custom keycap set

Lighting story

Under-glow LEDs are firmware-controlled through the same tooling used in BLT testing. Hydaelyn served as a demo platform for cross-board lighting macros and serial telemetry experiments.
