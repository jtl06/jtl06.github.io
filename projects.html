Firmware & Tooling

BLT latency measurement platform

Better Latency Tester (BLT) is a low-latency measurement rig that captures click-to-photon timings for PC games. Built around an STM32 Nucleo board, it blends USB HID emulation, DMA-driven ADC sampling, and microsecond timing to produce reproducible latency data.

  • Automatic or manual light-threshold calibration with photodiode and mic trigger modes.
  • Batch USB-triggered runs with onboard logging, Python GUI, and CSV exports.
  • Sample CS2 data shows Reflex shaving ~3 ms at 240 Hz; ideal for comparative testing.
