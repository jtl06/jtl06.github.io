EXT65

Completed in 2023, EXT65 is a custom extended 65% layout built to test machining tolerances and exposed fastener aesthetics. The chassis pairs electrophoresis-coated 6063 aluminum with mirror-polished stainless 304 weights to balance feel and finish.

  • 2023
  • 65% + nav
  • Top-mount
  • Dual weight
  • 6063 Al + stainless 304

Manufacturing lessons

EXT65 drove updates to my CAM toolpaths and fixture strategy. The dual-weight stack taught me how different metal compositions alter resonance—a lesson that feeds back into future firmware typing tests.
