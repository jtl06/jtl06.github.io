EXT65

The EXT65 features a custom extended 65% layout with a "southpaw" left handed numpad. The long stainless steel weight on the rear is mirror polished and engraved with a custom design.

  • 2023
  • 65% + nav
  • Sandwich gasket
  • 6063 Al · stainless 304
