LS70

The LS70 is a 70% layout board designed for a fan of the anime series "Code Geass." It utilizes a custom 70% PCB, featuring a unique symmetric layout. The solid brass bottom results a regal heft, with the board weighing in at 3.8kg (8.4lb) assembled.

  • 2022
  • 70% layout
  • O-ring mount
  • C268 brass · 6063 aluminum
