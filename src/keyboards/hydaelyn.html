Hydaelyn

Hydaelyn, themed and named after a character from the MMO Final Fantasy XIV, features a unique weight design with a slim copper weight on the back, and a seperate, internal weight to optimize space for the rear engraving while maintaining acoustics and heft.

  • TKL Layout
  • Top mount
  • 6063 Al · C110 copper
