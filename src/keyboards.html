Keyboard builds

I enjoy using beautiful tools, so I set out to build beautiful keyboards. Each board is custom designed and precisely machined from premium materials. Here are some highlights from 40+ builds across 14 unique designs.

LS70

Code Geass themed commission, utilizing a 5 piece design with a solid brass bottom.

Aria TKL

Personal design, featuring "Winkeyless" layout and a stunning pink finish.

NV70

NVIDIA themed board and metal keycaps, designed for a lifelong NVIDIAN to celebrate their success.

Hydaelyn

Final Fantasy XIV tribute, with an angular blue aluminum case.

EXT65

Hand polished stainless steel weight with laser engraving and custom southpaw layout.

Koi65

Koi-inspired duet of keyboards in two complementary color schemes.
