SwiftFIX — SIMD FIX pre-parser

C++20 FIX tag-value pre-parser for fast structural scanning before QuickFIX parsing.

SwiftFIX performs single-pass structural scanning, header indexing, body-length validation, and field-boundary extraction into a reusable zero-allocation offset table.

  • Improved frame-splitting throughput from 146 MiB/s in QuickFIX to 1.60 GiB/s scalar.
  • Reached 2.19 GiB/s with AVX2.
  • Reduced cycles per message from 3,999 to 257 while nearly eliminating L1D misses.
